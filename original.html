Capybara Dash

A simple runner; easy to play; designed so it can be driven by a single signal later. Tap/click or press Space to hop over jungle stuff; collect snacks; don’t faceplant.

For BCI/eye tracking later: set Input: Signal and feed a 0..1 value into Capybara.setSignal(v). Crossing the threshold triggers a jump with optional “hold” while the signal stays high.
